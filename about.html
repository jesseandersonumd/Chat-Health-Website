Meet Caring

Millions of youth across the world are experiencing HIV. Poor access to education, stigma toward people with HIV, and failure to use condoms are keeping youth in the dark. 

Start using Caring on Telegram by typing @caringhiv_bot

About HIV

There were approximately 36.7 million people worldwide living with HIV/AIDS at the end of 2016. Of these, 2.1 million were children less than 15 years old. Caring is here to change that. The lack of education about HIV around the world is alarming. 

Learn more about the HIV crisis from AVERT

A new way to teach

We also recognize that many educators want to teach their students about HIV and safer sex, but they may not have the materials to do so. With Caring, they do. ​​We've created over 20 materials that you can use in your classroom for free.
